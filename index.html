Why use active verbs?

Employers want to know what you can achieve for their company.
You need to say what you have contributed rather than simply what you have done.

How do you know you are using passive verbs in your resume? You could look online for a list of passive verbs and cross check you resume or cover letter or you can place your text in the editor above and it will not only check for passive verbs but also offer active verb suggestions.
